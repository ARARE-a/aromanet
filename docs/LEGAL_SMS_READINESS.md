## Before wider public launch

- Monitor `noreply@arare-ai.jp` as the temporary public contact address.
- Decide the official business operator name, address, phone number, and representative name.
- Add commercial transaction disclosure before paid plans, paid reservations, or settlement flows are introduced.
- Keep Twilio dashboard spend alerts enabled.
- Review Twilio SMS/Verify logs weekly during the demo period.

## SMS cost position

- Customers should not be charged an AromaNet-specific SMS verification fee.
- Their carrier may still charge normal SMS/data fees depending on their plan.
- AromaNet operator costs depend on Twilio Verify pricing, Japan SMS channel pricing, sender type, and exchange rates.

- https://www.twilio.com/en-us/verify/pricing
